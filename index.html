Super Knuckles Bros. (2018)

As I really like to revisualize some of my favourite older games, I started this one with a slight twist. Back in 2018 was a blown up meme which was called "Uganda Knuckles" of course stemming from a special, red Sonic the Hedgehog character. This meme had inspired all of my college projects to feature Uganda Knuckles that semester. My most favoured project was our final. We were to make any game we wanted.

This glorious monstrosity was created with wonky physics and features the first level of the original Super Mario Bros. Of course, with an alternative playable character that we all love.

Desert Crawler (2019)

After the 2018 Christmas break, my class recieved our first lab. We were to use a binary or XML serializer to save a load data into our game. I particularly liked the concept of a racing game. The goal of the game is to race through all of the objectives in the game, then race to the finish line. I implemented saving a loading profiles that had an associated name, current highest time, type of vehicle, and vehicle color. The race is on!
